📖 3. Reading Task

💼 Text: Exploring the World of Work

The world of work is diverse and constantly changing. People choose different careers based on their interests, skills, and opportunities. Some jobs require higher education, while others need practical experience.

Employees work in different environments such as offices, factories, or online platforms. Each job has its own responsibilities and requirements. Good communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills are important in most professions.

Today, the job market is becoming more competitive. Technology is changing the way people work, and new professions are appearing. That is why it is important to develop skills and gain experience to succeed in the modern workplace.
