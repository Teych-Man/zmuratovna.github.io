📖 3. Reading Task

🌐 Text: Life in the Oceans

Oceans are home to a vast diversity of life. Coral reefs, deep-sea vents, and open waters host countless species, each adapted to its environment. Marine predators, such as sharks, hunt prey to survive, while some animals rely on camouflage or speed to escape danger.

Human activity affects the oceans through pollution, overfishing, and climate change. Conservation efforts aim to protect marine ecosystems and maintain biodiversity. Understanding how animals live in the ocean helps scientists preserve these unique environments for the future.
