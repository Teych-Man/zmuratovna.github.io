📖 3. Reading Task

🎨 Text: Ways of Self-Expression

Self-expression is an important part of human life. It allows people to show their thoughts, feelings, and identity. There are many ways to express oneself, including art, music, writing, and fashion.

Young people often use social media as a platform for self-expression. They share ideas, opinions, and creative content. However, it is important to use self-expression responsibly and respectfully.

Creative activities such as drawing, dancing, and singing help people develop confidence and emotional intelligence. Self-expression also helps people understand themselves and others better.
