📖 3. Reading Task

🌐 Text: The World of Virtual Reality

Virtual reality (VR) is one of the most exciting technologies today. It allows people to enter digital environments that feel real and interactive. Users wear special headsets to explore virtual worlds.

VR is used not only for entertainment but also in education and medicine. For example, students can learn through simulations, and doctors can practice operations safely.

However, some experts believe that too much use of VR can cause problems such as addiction or social isolation. Despite this, VR continues to develop and become more popular.
