📖 3. Reading Task

👕 Text: The Journey of Clothes

Clothes go through a long journey before they reach consumers. First, materials such as cotton, wool, or synthetic fibers are produced. Then, these materials are sent to factories where clothes are designed and made.

After production, clothes are transported to stores around the world. Consumers buy them and use them in their daily lives. However, many clothes are thrown away after a short time.

Recycling and reusing clothes can help reduce waste and protect the environment. Sustainable fashion is becoming more popular as people become more aware of environmental problems.
