📖 3. Reading Task

🌈 Text: The Power of Creativity

Creativity is an important skill in the modern world. It helps people solve problems, express themselves, and develop new ideas. Creativity is not only important for artists but also for scientists, engineers, and students.

Imagination allows people to think beyond reality and create something new. Many successful inventions and discoveries began with a simple idea. Creative thinking helps people find different solutions and adapt to new situations.

There are many ways to develop creativity. People can read books, draw, write stories, or try new activities. Working in groups and sharing ideas can also inspire creativity. Everyone has creative potential, but it needs practice and development.
