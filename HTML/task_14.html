📖 3. Reading Task

🌿 Text: Describing Animal Features

When we study animals, it is important to describe their specific features. These include physical characteristics such as size, color, and body structure, as well as behavior and diet.

For example, elephants are large animals with strong bodies and long trunks. They live in groups and are known for their intelligence. Eagles have sharp vision and strong wings, which help them hunt and fly at high altitudes.

Presenting information about animals helps students develop speaking skills and learn how to organize information clearly. It also increases awareness about wildlife and the importance of protecting animals.
