📖 3. Reading Task

💼 Text: Tips for a Successful Interview

A job interview is an important step in getting a job. It gives the employer a chance to learn more about the applicant and their qualifications. Good preparation is key to success.

Before the interview, applicants should research the company and understand the job requirements. They should also practice answering common interview questions. Dressing appropriately and arriving on time are also important.

During the interview, it is important to be confident, polite, and honest. Clear communication and positive body language can help create a good impression. After the interview, it is a good idea to thank the employer for the opportunity.
