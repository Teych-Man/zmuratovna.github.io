📖 3. Reading Task

🌍 Text: Amazing Places Around the World

Our planet is full of amazing places that attract millions of tourists every year. Some of these places are natural wonders, such as mountains, lakes, and deserts. Others are man-made landmarks, like historical monuments and modern buildings.

For example, the Grand Canyon in the USA is famous for its impressive landscape, while the Great Wall of China is known for its historical importance. In Kazakhstan, places like Charyn Canyon and Burabay National Park are popular tourist destinations.

Traveling to different places helps people learn about new cultures, traditions, and ways of life. It also allows people to relax and enjoy beautiful views. Amazing places around the world continue to inspire people and create unforgettable memories.
