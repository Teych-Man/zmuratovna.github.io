📖 3. Reading Task

💼 Text: Choosing a Future Career

Choosing a future career is one of the most important decisions in a person’s life. It depends on interests, skills, education, and opportunities. Some people choose careers based on their passion, while others consider job stability and salary.

Education plays a key role in career development. Many professions require special qualifications and training. Work experience is also important because it helps people develop practical skills.

Today, the job market offers many opportunities, but it is also competitive. That is why it is important to set goals, improve skills, and continue learning. A good career choice can lead to personal satisfaction and success.
