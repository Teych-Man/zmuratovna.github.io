📖 3. Reading Task

🌐 Text: Life in the Future

The future is full of possibilities and exciting changes. Technology is developing rapidly, and it is expected to transform many aspects of our lives. Artificial intelligence, robots, and digital systems will become more common in everyday life.

In the future, people may live in smart homes that can control lighting, temperature, and security automatically. Transportation will also change, with electric cars and flying vehicles becoming more popular. Education may become more flexible, with online learning and virtual classrooms.

However, these changes also bring challenges. People may worry about losing jobs due to automation or becoming too dependent on technology. Despite this, many believe that the future will offer new opportunities and improve the quality of life.
