📖 3. Reading Task

🌙 Text: The Legend of the Vanishing Woman

Across small towns, people often tell mysterious stories. One famous legend is about a woman who appears on empty roads at night. She asks drivers for help and then suddenly disappears.

Some witnesses say they feel a cold wind when she vanishes. Others believe she warns people about danger. There is no scientific evidence to prove this story, but many people still believe it.

Whether true or not, the legend continues to live in people’s imagination.

✔ Task 1. True / False
  1. The woman appears during the day.
  2. She disappears suddenly.
  3. There is scientific proof of the story.
  4. Some people believe the legend.
  5. The story is completely forgotten.
💬 Task 2. Answer the questions
  1. What happens when the woman appears?
  2. What do witnesses say?
  3. Is there any real evidence?
  4. Why do people continue to tell this story?
🔎 Task 3. Vocabulary from context

Find words in the text that mean:

  1. people who saw something
  2. proof
  3. suddenly disappear

📚 4. Grammar Practice

✏ Past Simple vs Past Continuous

Choose the correct form.

  1. The driver (saw / was seeing) the woman on the road.
  2. They (were traveling / traveled) at night when it happened.
  3. She (stood / was standing) near the road.
  4. The witness (was driving / drove) home.
  5. People (talked / were talking) about the legend for years.
