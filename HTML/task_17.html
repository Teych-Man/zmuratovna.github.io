📖 3. Reading Task

⏳ Text: From Sundials to Smart Clocks

People have measured time for thousands of years. Early timekeeping devices included sundials, which used the position of the sun to show time. Another simple device was the hourglass, which measured time using sand.

Later, mechanical clocks were invented. These clocks used gears and moving parts to measure time more accurately. They became popular in Europe during the Middle Ages.

Today, digital clocks and smart devices are widely used. They are very accurate and easy to use. Modern technology has made timekeeping more precise and convenient than ever before.
