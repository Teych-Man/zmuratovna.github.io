📖 3. Reading Task

💼 Text: Keys to Business Success

Success in business depends on many factors. One of the most important is having a clear idea and a strong strategy. Entrepreneurs must understand the market and identify customer needs.

Good management is also essential. A successful company provides high-quality products or services and maintains good relationships with customers. Communication and teamwork play a key role in business development.

However, business always involves risk. Not all ideas become successful, but learning from mistakes helps entrepreneurs grow. Innovation and adaptability are also important in a changing world.
