📖 3. Reading Task

🌿 Text: Teenagers and Their Challenges

Teenagers today face many challenges in their daily lives. School pressure, social expectations, and relationships with friends can create stress and anxiety. One of the most common problems is peer pressure, when teenagers feel forced to follow others to be accepted.

Bullying is another serious issue. It can happen both in real life and online. Victims of bullying often feel lonely and lose confidence. That is why it is important to talk about problems and ask for help.

Support from family, teachers, and friends plays a key role in solving these issues. Good communication can help teenagers express their feelings and find solutions. Learning how to manage problems is an important step in becoming a confident and independent person.

📚 4. Grammar Practice

✏ Present Simple vs Present Continuous

Choose the correct form:

  1. Teenagers often (face / are facing) many problems.
  2. She (feels / is feeling) stressed before exams.
  3. They (talk / are talking) about their problems now.
  4. Bullying (affects / is affecting) students’ confidence.
  5. He (tries / is trying) to solve his problems at the moment.
