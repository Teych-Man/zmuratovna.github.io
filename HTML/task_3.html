📖 3. Reading Task

🌐 Text: The Debate Around Social Media

Social media is one of the most controversial topics today. Many people use it to communicate, share ideas, and stay informed. However, it also has negative sides.

Some experts believe that social media causes addiction and spreads false information. Others think it helps people learn, communicate, and express themselves freely.

Different countries have different opinions and rules about social media. The discussion continues as people try to find a balance between its advantages and disadvantages.

✔ Task 1. True / False
  1. Social media is not a controversial topic.
  2. People use social media to communicate.
  3. Some experts think it has negative effects.
  4. All countries have the same opinion.
  5. People are still discussing this issue.
💬 Task 2. Answer the questions
  1. Why is social media controversial?
  2. What are the advantages of social media?
  3. What problems does it create?
