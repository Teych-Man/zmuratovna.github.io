📖 3. Reading Task

💼 Text: Learning from Interviews

Analyzing an interview is an important step in improving communication skills. After an interview, it is useful to review your answers, behavior, and overall performance.

One important part of analysis is identifying strengths and weaknesses. For example, a person may speak clearly but feel nervous. Another may have good ideas but need better organization in answers.

Feedback from teachers, employers, or peers can help identify areas for improvement. By practicing and learning from mistakes, people can become more confident and successful in future interviews.
