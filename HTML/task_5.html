📖 3. Reading Task

🌠 Text: The Importance of Space Exploration

Space exploration has always fascinated humanity. For centuries, people have looked at the stars and wondered about the universe. Today, scientists use advanced technology such as spacecraft 🚀 and telescopes 🔭 to explore space.

Space missions help scientists learn more about planets 🪐, stars ⭐, and galaxies 🌌. For example, satellites orbiting Earth collect important data about weather, climate change, and communication systems. Space exploration also helps researchers understand whether life might exist on other planets.

However, space missions are expensive and sometimes dangerous. Astronauts 👨‍🚀 must train for many years before traveling to space. Despite these challenges, many countries continue to invest in space programs because they believe space exploration will bring new discoveries and technological progress.
