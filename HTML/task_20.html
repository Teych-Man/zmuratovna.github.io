📖 3. Reading Task

🌍 Text: A Changing World

Our world is constantly changing due to human activity and technological development. One of the biggest challenges today is climate change. It affects weather patterns, ecosystems, and people’s lives.

Pollution is another serious issue. Air, water, and land pollution can harm human health and damage the environment. Many countries are trying to reduce pollution and use cleaner energy sources.

Technology also plays an important role in shaping the modern world. It can help solve environmental problems, but it can also create new challenges. It is important to find a balance between development and environmental protection.
