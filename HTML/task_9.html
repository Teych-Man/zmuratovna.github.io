📖 3. Reading Task

🌟 Text: The Joy of Reading

Reading is one of the most enjoyable and useful activities. It allows people to explore new worlds, learn new ideas, and improve their language skills. Books can be both entertaining and educational.

Many people read for pleasure in their free time. Some prefer novels, while others enjoy short stories, magazines, or online articles. Reading helps develop imagination and creativity. It also improves vocabulary and critical thinking skills.

Libraries and digital platforms make books more accessible than ever before. Even with modern technology, reading remains an important habit that supports personal and academic growth.
