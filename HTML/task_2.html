📖 3. Reading Task

🌟 Text: Following Your Dreams

Everyone has dreams and goals in life. Dreams help people imagine their future and give them motivation to move forward. Some dreams are simple, while others are big and ambitious.

To achieve dreams, people need to work hard and believe in themselves. Setting clear goals and making a plan are important steps. Support from family and friends can also help people succeed.

Sometimes people face difficulties, but they should not give up. Learning from mistakes and staying positive can help turn dreams into reality.
