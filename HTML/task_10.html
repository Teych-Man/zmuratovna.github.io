📖 3. Reading Task

🌍 Text: Ways of Living Around the World

People around the world live in many different ways. Lifestyle depends on culture, environment, and personal choices. Some people prefer living in big cities, while others enjoy the спокойствие of rural areas.

Urban life offers more opportunities, such as jobs, education, and entertainment. However, it can also be stressful and fast-paced. Rural life, on the other hand, is usually quieter and closer to nature, but it may have fewer services and opportunities.

Cultural traditions also play an important role in shaping people’s lifestyles. Food, clothing, celebrations, and daily routines can vary greatly from one country to another. Understanding different ways of living helps people become more open-minded and respectful.
